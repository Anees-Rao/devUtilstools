Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Data collected

DevUtils collects minimal data. We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymized page view data (pages visited, session duration, browser type, country-level location). We also use Google AdSense to serve advertisements, which may set cookies.

2. Tool data — client-side only

All tool inputs and outputs are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Text, files, JSON, timestamps, or any other data you enter into a tool is never sent to our servers. We have no access to what you type or paste into any tool.

3. Cookies

We use cookies for Google Analytics (analytics cookies) and Google AdSense (advertising cookies). You can opt out of Google Analytics via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. AdSense cookies are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-party services

We use Google Analytics 4 and Google AdSense. These services have their own privacy policies which govern their data use. We are not responsible for their data practices.

5. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: privacy@devutils.com
